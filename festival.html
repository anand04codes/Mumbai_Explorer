Festivals in Mumbai

Experience the vibrant festivals celebrated in Mumbai, showcasing the city's rich culture and traditions.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Celebrated in August/September

A grand festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha, marked by vibrant processions and elaborate decorations.

Diwali

Celebrated in October/November

The festival of lights, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness, celebrated with fireworks and family gatherings.

Holi

Celebrated in March

The festival of colors, where people throw colored powders and water at each other, celebrating love and joy.

Eid

Celebrated in August/September

A significant Islamic festival marked by prayers, feasting, and community gatherings, celebrating the end of Ramadan.

Navratri

Celebrated in September/October

A nine-night festival dedicated to the worship of the goddess Durga, celebrated with dance and music.

Christmas

Celebrated on December 25

A festive celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ, marked by decorations, carols, and family gatherings.
